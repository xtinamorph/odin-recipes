The best pancakes

Fluffy pancakes is the best way to start your day. These pancakes are light and fluffy with soft centers and gently crisp edges. They have a mild sweetness that pairs perfectly with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or a dollop of butter. Each bite is tender and comforting, making them ideal for a cozy weekend breakfast or a quick weekday treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup app-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the milk, egg, melted butter and vanilla.
  3. Pour he wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Stir until just combined. Make sure not to over mix.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. lightly grease with butter or oil
  5. pour 1/4 cup of batter per pancake into the pan. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden.
  6. stack them and serve with butter a syrup
