A Perfect Taco Night

This taco repipe is great for a party of just a fun night with the family. These tacos are very customizable and can be prepared with any meat of your choice. It is very fun to mix and match the various toppings and build the most delicous taco. Each taco is the perfect size, you could easily eat half a dozen of them

I can still remember the first time I had street tacos. I was having a sleepover and my mother needed a way to feed all of my friends. Thats when she remembered the time that she was in mexico in college and had 20 street tacos at once. Thats when she knew that this was the recipe that she had to make that night. Later on she passed this recipe on to me, and I now make these tacos for my family all the time.

Ingredients

  • 1 carne asada steak
  • 1-2 avocados
  • 12 (4-inch)Tortillas
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 Jalapeno pepper sliced
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup cumbled cotija cheese
  • limes

Instructions

  1. Grill the carne asada according to your preference
  2. Slice the carne asade into thin strips
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork. Stir in salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lime juice
  4. Fill each tortilla with a spread of mashed avocade and a scoop of meat
  5. Top the tacos off with fresh cilantro, jalapenos, onions, and cotija cheese. Squeeze lime juice over the top to finish
